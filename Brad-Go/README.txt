This HotelProgram does the following:

With a slice of 8 prenamed hotels, their ratings over the span of 10 years are randomly generated. 
-Each rating can be within 1-100.
--HOWEVER, up to 5 of the first years can have a rating of N/A. The number of empty years is chosen randomly.
--Empty years are to show that the hotel did not open yet.

Once all ratings are generated, the program ten runs tests on the slice of hotels.
-To be a part of the best hotels, the hotel must have a rating of 95 or above. 
-To be a part of the worst hotels, the hotel must have the most amount of low ratings out of every other hotel.
